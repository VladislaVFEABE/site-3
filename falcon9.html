Falcon 9

a reusable two-stage medium-lift launch vehicle designed
and manufactured by SpaceX in the United States.
Both the first and second stages are powered by SpaceX Merlin engines
that use cryogenic liquid oxygen and rocket kerosene (RP-1) as fuel.
